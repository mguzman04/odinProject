Crepes Recipe

Description

Crepes are delicious

Ingredients

Yields 12 servings:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flout
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted

Directions

  1. Step 1

    Sift together flour, sugar, and salt; set aside. Beat eggs and milk together in a large owl with an electric mixer. Beat in flour mixture until smooth; stir in melted butter.

  2. Step 2

    Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat

  3. Step 3

    Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 2 tablespoons for each crepe. Tip and rotate pan to spread batter as thinly as possible. Brown on both sides. Repeat with remaining batter. Server hot.
